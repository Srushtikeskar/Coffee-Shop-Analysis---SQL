# Coffee-Shop-Analysis---SQL
Analysing the Coffee Shop sales to understand business outcomes and requirements

## Business Objective

The objective of this Coffee Shop Sales Analysis project is to use SQL to evaluate sales performance across products, customers, and cities in order to identify the key factors driving revenue. The primary goal is to understand customer purchasing behavior, measure average customer spending, and determine which locations contribute the highest sales to the business. A major focus of the analysis is to identify the **top three cities generating the highest coffee shop revenue**, enabling the business to recognize its strongest markets and make informed decisions about expansion, marketing, and resource allocation. By transforming raw transactional data into actionable insights, this project aims to solve the problem of limited visibility into city-level sales performance and support data-driven business growth strategies.

## What This Project Aims to Solve

This project aims to solve the challenge of understanding **which cities, customers, and products contribute most to overall coffee shop revenue**. Many businesses collect large volumes of transactional data but lack clear visibility into where sales performance is strongest and which customer segments generate the most value. By analyzing sales data using SQL, this project evaluates customer spending patterns, measures product performance, and identifies revenue trends across locations. A key objective is to **identify the top 3 highest-performing cities** based on sales, helping the business recognize its strongest markets and support data-driven decisions for expansion, marketing, and revenue optimization.
